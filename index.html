About Connectics

Founded in 2015, Connectics emerged from a vision to democratize enterprise-grade telecommunication solutions for growing businesses. Our founders, with over 30 years of combined experience in telecommunications and IT infrastructure, recognized the need for flexible, scalable communication solutions that wouldn't break the bank for small and medium-sized enterprises.

Our mission is to empower businesses with cutting-edge communication tools that drive growth and efficiency. We believe in delivering not just technology, but true partnerships with our clients, guided by our core values of innovation, reliability, and exceptional service. Every solution we provide is backed by our commitment to excellence and our understanding that effective communication is the backbone of business success.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does the implementation process take?

Most implementations are completed within 5-10 business days, depending on system complexity and customization requirements. Our experienced team ensures a smooth transition with minimal disruption to your operations.

What kind of support do you provide?

We offer 24/7 technical support through multiple channels including phone, email, and live chat. Our support team is based locally and provides rapid response times with a 98% first-call resolution rate.

Can your systems integrate with our existing software?

Yes, our solutions integrate seamlessly with most popular business applications including Salesforce, Microsoft 365, Google Workspace, and major CRM platforms. Custom integrations are also available.

What happens if we need to scale up?

Our flexible architecture allows for easy scaling. You can add users, features, or capacity instantly through our self-service portal. No hardware changes or system downtime required.

How do you ensure system security?

We implement multiple layers of security including end-to-end encryption, regular security audits, and compliance with industry standards like GDPR and ISO 27001. All data centers are Tier 4 certified.
